Delete Your DocXpress Account

Complete Guide to Account Deletion and Data Removal

Overview

At DocXpress, we believe in empowering users with full control over their personal data. This page provides complete instructions on how to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Important: Account deletion is a permanent action and cannot be undone. Please read this entire guide before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your DocXpress account using one of two methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the quickest way to delete your account:

1 Open the DocXpress App

Launch the DocXpress mobile application on your device.

2 Navigate to Settings

Tap the menu icon (☰) or open "Settings" from the navigation menu.

3 Go to Profile

Select "Profile" or "Account Settings" from the settings menu.

4 Select Delete Account

Scroll down and tap the Delete Account button (usually at the bottom).

5 Confirm Action

A confirmation dialog will appear. Read the warning and tap Delete to confirm.

6 Enter Your Password

For security purposes, you must enter your DocXpress account password to confirm the deletion.

7 Account Deleted

Your account and all associated data will be immediately deleted. You will be logged out of the app.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, you can contact us directly:

Email: support@docxpress.app

Subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Include: Your registered email address and account username

Response Time: We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

Data Type What Gets Deleted Deletion Timeline
Account Information Name, email address, password, account ID Immediately
App Ratings Any ratings or reviews you submitted Immediately
User Activity Log Login history, feature usage data Immediately
Cloud Sync Data Any settings or preferences stored on our servers Immediately
Authentication Tokens JWT tokens and session data Immediately

Note: Temporary files from document conversions are automatically deleted immediately after download. We do not retain copies of documents you convert.

What Data Is NOT Deleted (Lawful Reasons)

Due to legal requirements, the following may be retained:

  • System Logs: Anonymized server logs and analytics data for up to 90 days (cannot be traced back to you)
  • Legal Compliance: Data required to comply with laws, regulations, or legal obligations
  • Fraud Prevention: Information necessary to detect and prevent fraud or security incidents

This retained data is not personally identifiable and cannot be used to contact you or identify you directly.

Important Information Before Deleting

⚠️ This Action Is Permanent

Account deletion cannot be reversed. All your data, including purchase history, preferences, and account information, will be permanently deleted from our systems. Make sure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

⚠️ Email Address Cannot Be Reused Immediately

Once deleted, your email address will be held for 30 days before it can be used to create a new account. This is a security measure to protect your privacy.

⚠️ Subscription and Payment Methods

If you have an active subscription, it will be canceled upon account deletion. No refunds will be issued for unused portions of your subscription.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I recover my account after deleting it?
A: No, account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. Once deleted, all your data is permanently removed from our servers. If you wish to use DocXpress again, you will need to create a new account.
Q: How long does it take for my data to be deleted?
A: Personal account data is deleted immediately upon confirmation. System logs and analytics data may persist for up to 90 days but are anonymized and cannot identify you.
Q: Will my documents be deleted?
A: Yes. Any documents you uploaded or processed will be deleted. Additionally, temporary files from conversions are already deleted immediately after download, so they won't be retained.
Q: Can I delete only some of my data without deleting my account?
A: Currently, DocXpress requires full account deletion to remove data. You cannot selectively delete individual data types. If you want to delete only specific information, please contact our support team for assistance.
Q: What if I forgot my password?
A: If you cannot access your account due to a forgotten password, you can use the "Forgot Password" option to reset it. Alternatively, contact support@docxpress.app to request account deletion.
Q: Is my data secure during the deletion process?
A: Yes. All data transmission is encrypted with HTTPS/SSL. Password verification adds an additional layer of security to ensure only you can delete your account.
Q: How long can I use my account before deciding to delete it?
A: You can delete your account at any time. There is no minimum usage period. If you delete your account, it will have no impact on other users or the app's functionality.
